Deviled Eggs

Description:

Eggs and mayo yum yum yum! Get these in my tummy fast! I'm so excited to eat some deviled eggs.

Recipe taken from allrecipes.com

Ingredients:

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 Tb mayonmaise
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp prepared mustard
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tb finely chopped onion
  • 1 Tb finely chopped celery
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside.
  2. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl.
  3. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, onion, and celery; mix well.
  4. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.
